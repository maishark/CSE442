Chatbot with Eye Gesture Cursor Interaction

Our aim:

Our project, "Chatbot with Eye Gesture Cursor Interaction," aims to empower individuals with disabilities by enabling them to interact with chatbots using only their eyes.

How to use:

  • Move your right eye for moving the cursor
  • Make Selections by a simple blink of the left eye
